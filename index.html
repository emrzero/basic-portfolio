About Me

A little hummingbird visits me every day. It's got a rust-colored chest with emerald-green tail feathers. It sits on the same wire, with the same pose, and the same side to side glances. It promptly leaves after I glance up at it, as if saying "You saw me? Good. Adios!" The moment lasts no more than 10 seconds. It's my favorite part of the day.
